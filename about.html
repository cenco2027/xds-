Our Story

XDS designs, engineers, and manufactures bicycles at global scale—with over 8 million bikes produced annually in our vertically integrated facilities. Every frame, weld, and assembly is built in-house, using advanced automation and precision testing to ensure consistency, performance, and long-term value.

From Olympic athletes to daily commuters, our bikes are trusted by riders around the world—because we don't just build for volume. We build for quality, efficiency, and the ride itself.

Our Story XDS 1

At XDS, we've spent three decades refining how bicycles are built by controlling every step of the process, from carbon layup to final torque check. While many brands rely on third parties, we've kept production under one roof to ensure consistency and quality at every stage.

This approach goes beyond quality control. It allows us to fine-tune geometry, optimize materials, and deliver bikes that perform across categories and price points. It's how we bring together performance, value, and scale in a way that works for both riders and retailers.
